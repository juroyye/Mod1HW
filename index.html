Chapter One: The Escape

The year is 4067. As the renowned commander of the Renegade, you’re tasked with transporting a dark energy tesseract from the star system of Andretti to the star system of Fúsille, where your team of virtuous vigilantes will have it properly destroyed.

However, the malicious Lord Scarr and his battalion will stop at nothing to ensure he acquires this tesseract. Such power at his hands can spell disaster for the solar system, and even worse, the universe…
